There is nothing quite like curling up under a handmade blanket. This chunky throw works up quickly and makes a stunning addition to any room.

## Materials Needed

- **Yarn**: 6 skeins of super bulky (6) yarn, approximately 900 yards total
- **Hook**: 12mm (P/Q) crochet hook
- **Notions**: Yarn needle, scissors

## Finished Size

Approximately 50 inches x 60 inches

## Gauge

8 dc = 4 inches

## Pattern Instructions

### Foundation Row

Ch 101.

### Row 1

Dc in 4th ch from hook (counts as first dc), dc in each ch across. (99 dc)

### Row 2

Ch 3 (counts as dc), turn, dc in each st across.

### Rows 3-75

Repeat Row 2.

### Finishing

Fasten off and weave in all ends.

## Optional Border

Work 1 round of sc evenly around the entire blanket, working 3 sc in each corner.

## Tips for Success

- Use a yarn with good stitch definition for best results
- Block your finished blanket for a polished look
- Consider using multiple colors for stripes

Enjoy your cozy new blanket!
