This sweet little bunny is one of my most requested patterns! With floppy ears and an adorable face, it makes the perfect gift for little ones.

## Materials Needed

- **Yarn**: Worsted weight cotton or acrylic
  - Main color: 100g (cream, gray, or brown)
  - Accent: Small amount for inner ears (pink)
  - Small amount of black for eyes
- **Hook**: 3.5mm (E) crochet hook
- **Notions**: Polyester fiberfill, safety eyes (12mm), yarn needle, stitch marker

## Finished Size

Approximately 10 inches tall when seated

## Abbreviations

- **MR** - Magic ring
- **sc** - Single crochet
- **inc** - Increase (2 sc in one stitch)
- **dec** - Invisible decrease
- **st(s)** - Stitch(es)

## Pattern

### Head

**Rnd 1:** 6 sc in MR (6)

**Rnd 2:** Inc in each st around (12)

**Rnd 3:** *Sc 1, inc* repeat around (18)

**Rnd 4:** *Sc 2, inc* repeat around (24)

**Rnd 5:** *Sc 3, inc* repeat around (30)

**Rnd 6-10:** Sc in each st around (30)

Insert safety eyes between rounds 7-8, about 6 stitches apart.

**Rnd 11:** *Sc 3, dec* repeat around (24)

**Rnd 12:** *Sc 2, dec* repeat around (18)

Stuff firmly.

**Rnd 13:** *Sc 1, dec* repeat around (12)

**Rnd 14:** Dec around (6)

Fasten off and close the hole.

### Body

**Rnd 1:** 6 sc in MR (6)

**Rnd 2:** Inc in each st around (12)

**Rnd 3:** *Sc 1, inc* repeat around (18)

**Rnd 4:** *Sc 2, inc* repeat around (24)

**Rnd 5-12:** Sc in each st around (24)

**Rnd 13:** *Sc 2, dec* repeat around (18)

Stuff body.

**Rnd 14:** *Sc 1, dec* repeat around (12)

Fasten off, leaving a long tail for sewing.

### Ears (make 2)

**Rnd 1:** 6 sc in MR (6)

**Rnd 2:** *Sc 1, inc* repeat around (9)

**Rnd 3-12:** Sc in each st around (9)

Fasten off, leaving tail for sewing. Do not stuff.

### Assembly

1. Sew head to body
2. Attach ears to top of head
3. Embroider nose and mouth
4. Add any finishing touches

Your bunny is complete! Give it lots of love.
